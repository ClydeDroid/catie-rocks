More

I've spent the past four years getting to know Catie. She has enriched my life in more ways than I can count. Yet somehow, she continues to find ways to surprise me.

This website is a tribute to her. I want the world to know how awesome she is. I need to world to know how lucky I am to be with such a wonderful woman. So here are just a few of the ways that she kicks ass.

She's a Culinary Innovator

She isn't just good at finding tasty recipes (and she has found many). Catie has the ability to break from the instructions and improvise. She can work with whatever we've got in the pantry and make something yummy. Cooking with her is basically my favorite thing to do!

She's an Incredible Student

Catie graduated from Johns Hopkins University with a 3.97 GPA. Yes, you read that right. 3.97. Who does that? Who else could do so well at such a respected university?

She's Gorgeous

Oh my god, I don't know how a chump like me ended up with such a beauty, but I'm hoping she won't notice anytime soon so I can keep looking at those lovely eyes!

She's Compassionate

Catie studied Public Health in school because she cares about preventing disease, especially in those people who might not have the means to protect themselves. Her time spent volunteering for the Baltimore Needle Exchange opened up my eyes to the power of needle exchanges to prevent the spread of disease among injection drug users.

In her current job as a Research Assistant, she continues to work with that population, doing what she can to help out some of our poorest and most forgotten souls.

She also introduced me to a great volunteering opportunity at an organization called Moveable Feast. They are a meal delivery service for people in Maryland with diseases like HIV who are too sick to be able to cook for themselves. Here we are making cookies for them! They really are a great organization. Check them our here!

She's Fun

We do so much fun stuff together. Whether it's going to shows (her taste in music is impeccable), hiking in the woods for miles and miles, exploring new cities, playing in the snow, or just laying on the couch binging on Netflix, I'm always having fun because of her!

She Gives Great Gifts

She's definitely the best gift giver I know. To name just a few she's given me: a manual coffee grinder (I'm a coffee lover), little finger puppets of Plato and Nietzsche (I'm a philosophy nerd), a beautiful handmade journal, and this record player. I'm trying to become as good as her, but I don't think I'm there yet!

She's the Best Partner I Could Ever Ask For

Before I met Catie, I never really understood what partnership meant. She has taught me what it means to truly care for someone else. She has taught me thoughtfulness, kindness, and selflessness. She has shown me what true love feels like, and it is like nothing I have ever felt before.

We have grown so much since we first met. From getting through college, to getting our first jobs, moving apartments, and raising our adorable kitties, we have forged an incredible life together.

I hope to God I don't screw things up, because there's no one else in this world I would rather spend my life with. I wouldn't trade anything for the privilege I have of knowing this woman. She's the smartest, funniest, most beautiful girl I've ever met, and I am madly, madly in love with her.

I hope that someday you, kind stranger, will also have the chance to meet Catie.
